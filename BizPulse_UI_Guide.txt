DESIGN LANGUAGE OVERVIEW
  Theme         : AI-first, light intelligence, data clarity
  Primary vibe  : Modern fintech meets AI assistant — clean, trustworthy, smart
  Color system  : Light blue gradient base (#E8F4FD → #C2E0F8 → #90C7F0)
                  Deep navy accents (#0A2540, #1A3A5C)
                  AI purple highlights (#6C63FF, #8B7CF6)
                  Success green (#00C896)
                  Warning amber (#FFB020)
                  Danger red (#FF4757)
                  White surfaces (#FFFFFF, #F8FBFF)
  Gradients     : Backgrounds use subtle top-to-bottom or diagonal gradients
                  Cards use glass-morphism: white at 90% opacity, soft border
                  Hero sections: deep navy (#0A2540) to AI blue (#1E6FCC)
                  AI elements: purple-to-blue (#6C63FF → #3B9FE8)
  Typography    : Headlines — SF Pro Display Bold / Inter Bold, 28–32px
                  Subheadlines — SF Pro Display Semibold / Inter Semibold, 18–22px
                  Body — SF Pro Text / Inter Regular, 14–16px
                  Numbers/metrics — SF Pro Display Bold, tabular figures, 24–40px
                  Labels — Inter Medium, 11–12px, letter-spacing 0.5px, uppercase
  Spacing       : 16px base grid. Cards have 16px internal padding.
                  Section gaps: 24px. Screen horizontal padding: 20px.
  Corners       : Cards — 16px radius. Buttons — 12px radius. Pills — 20px radius.
                  Bottom sheet — 24px top radius.
  Shadows       : Cards — 0 4px 20px rgba(10,37,64,0.08)
                  Elevated modals — 0 8px 40px rgba(10,37,64,0.16)
  AI signature  : Every AI-generated element has a subtle left border or badge
                  in gradient purple-to-blue (#6C63FF → #3B9FE8) with a
                  small sparkle/star icon (✦) prefix.

UX PRINCIPLES (scientifically proven — applied throughout)
  1. Glanceability first — business owners check the app in 10-second bursts.
     Every screen's most important number must be visible without scrolling.
  2. Progressive disclosure — show the summary, tap to drill down. Never dump
     all data on one screen.
  3. Ambient awareness — the app should feel "alive." Subtle animations,
     live tickers, and pulsing indicators signal that data is current.
  4. Actionability over information — every insight must have a clear next
     action. No data without a decision attached.
  5. Trust signals — always show when data was last synced. Owners need to
     know the numbers are real and current.
  6. Cognitive load reduction — use color, icons, and position to communicate
     status before text. A red badge means danger before the owner reads it.
  7. Habitual design — morning push notification creates a daily ritual.
     The home screen is designed to be the first thing opened each day.

================================================================================

PROMPT BLOCK FOR STITCH AI:
"Design a splash screen for BizPulse mobile app. Full screen deep navy-to-AI-blue
gradient background (#0A2540 at top transitioning to #1E6FCC at bottom). Dead
center: the BizPulse logo — a stylized waveform or pulse line that forms a
letter B, in white, 80px wide. Below the logo: the wordmark 'BizPulse' in white

progress bar, 160px wide, with rounded ends, showing loading progress in white
at 60% fill. Below progress bar: 'Connecting to your data...' in white 12px,
opacity 50%. Subtle animated particles or dots floating in the background to
convey AI intelligence. No other UI elements."

DESIGN DETAILS:
  Background      : Full bleed gradient — #0A2540 (top) → #1E6FCC (bottom)
  Logo position   : Vertical center - 40px (slightly above true center)
  Logo            : Pulse/waveform icon forming 'B', white, 80x80px
  Wordmark        : 'BizPulse', white, Inter Bold, 28px, 8px below logo
  Tagline         : 'Your business, always awake.', white 70% opacity,
                    Inter Regular, 14px, 6px below wordmark
  Progress bar    : 160px wide, 3px tall, white 30% bg, white fill, rounded
                    Position: 48px above bottom safe area
  Loading text    : 'Connecting to your data...' white 50%, 12px, 8px above bar
  Particles       : 8-12 small white dots (4-8px) scattered, low opacity (15-25%)
  Animation note  : Logo pulses gently. Progress bar fills left to right.

PURPOSE: Sets the brand tone immediately. Navy-to-blue gradient signals
intelligence and trust. The pulse icon metaphor — the app is monitoring
the business heartbeat.
