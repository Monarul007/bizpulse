1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
________________________________________________________________________________

BizPulse is a mobile-first AI business intelligence platform that plugs into any
existing business system (MySQL/Laravel, or any database) and gives the business
owner a real-time, conversational view of their entire operation — from sales and
inventory to customers, employees, and finances.

The core insight: Business owners today are rich in data and poor in answers.
They have custom admin systems, sales reports, spreadsheets — but no single place
where they can ask "How is my business doing right now?" and get an instant,
intelligent response.

BizPulse is not another admin panel. It is a Smart Business Pod — an AI co-pilot
that sits on the owner's phone, monitors everything, alerts them proactively, and
answers questions in plain language, day or night.

Pilot client: eMartway Skincare Limited, Bangladesh's largest beauty e-commerce
platform, with 300+ brands, 1,000+ SKUs, and operations across Bangladesh.

Long-term goal: A white-label SaaS product deployable for any business type —
e-commerce, restaurants, clinics, law firms — without changing the client's
existing systems.

2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
________________________________________________________________________________

A typical business owner in Bangladesh (and globally) faces the following daily
operational challenges:

  - They must log into multiple systems to get a full picture of the business.
  - Sales reports are generated manually or periodically, not in real time.
  - Dead stock, churn risk, and revenue drops are discovered too late.
  - Valuable customers leave silently with no early warning system.
  - Employee performance is tracked informally or not at all.
  - Financial clarity (P&L, margins, cash flow) requires an accountant or
    manual spreadsheet work.
  - There is no way to "ask the business" a question and get an instant answer.

The result: The business owner is always reactive, never proactive. They make
decisions based on gut feeling rather than real-time intelligence.

BizPulse solves this by converting raw business data into live, conversational,
actionable intelligence — delivered to the owner's phone.

4. TARGET USERS
________________________________________________________________________________

PRIMARY USER — The Business Owner / Managing Director

  Profile:
    - Owns or runs a small-to-medium business (10–500 employees)
    - Has an existing operational system (website, admin panel, POS, ERP)
    - Is time-poor and cannot manually check dashboards regularly
    - Makes final decisions on pricing, staffing, promotions, and strategy
    - Uses a smartphone as their primary communication device
    - Does not have a dedicated BI analyst or data team

  Core Need:
    To know the real-time health of their business — sales, customers,
    inventory, team, finances — with minimal effort, from anywhere.

SECONDARY USER — Operations Manager / Senior Staff

  Profile:
    - Manages day-to-day operations on behalf of the owner
    - Needs departmental insights: logistics, customer service, inventory
    - Role-based access (cannot see financials unless permitted)

TERTIARY USER — Investors / Silent Partners (future)

  Profile:
    - Needs read-only high-level reporting
    - Monthly/quarterly financial digest access

5. PRODUCT SCOPE
________________________________________________________________________________

IN SCOPE (MVP — Version 1.0):

  - Read-only connector to client's MySQL database
  - Data sync engine (Laravel Scheduler + Queues, every 5 minutes or webhook-triggered)
  - 6 core intelligence modules (detailed in Section 6)
  - React Native mobile app via Expo (iOS + Android)
  - AI co-pilot with natural language query ("Ask your business anything")
  - Push notifications for proactive alerts
  - Biometric login (fingerprint / Face ID)
  - Offline mode (last-synced data available without internet)
  - Multi-tenant architecture (one BizPulse deployment serves many clients)
  - PDF report auto-generation (monthly financial digest)

OUT OF SCOPE (MVP):

  - Writing back to the client's database (read-only only in v1)
  - Native POS or order management system
  - Full ERP replacement
  - Direct payment processing
  - White-label app customization (v2 feature)
  - Voice input (v2 feature)

6. CORE FEATURES & MODULES
________________________________________________________________________________

MODULE 1 — SALES & REVENUE INTELLIGENCE

  Real-time revenue ticker (updates every 60 seconds)
  Revenue vs. yesterday, last week, last month comparison
  Hourly sales heatmap (best hours and days by revenue)
  Revenue forecasting (ML model predicts end-of-month target)
  Anomaly alerts ("Sales dropped 40% in the last 2 hours")
  Coupon and promotion ROI tracking
  Refund and return rate analysis per product
  Geographic sales map (district-level in Bangladesh)
  Channel attribution (app vs. website vs. Facebook vs. direct)

MODULE 2 — INVENTORY & SUPPLY CHAIN INTELLIGENCE

  Dead stock radar (items with zero sales in 30+ days, capital at risk in BDT)
  Reorder intelligence (stockout date prediction per SKU based on sell velocity)
  Margin per SKU (profit after logistics cost)
  Seasonal demand pattern detection
  Bundle opportunity finder (products bought together → suggest combos)
  Supplier performance tracking (late deliveries, quality complaints)
  Price elasticity modeling per SKU
  Warehouse aging report (FIFO compliance, expiry risk)

MODULE 3 — CUSTOMER INTELLIGENCE & CRM LAYER

  RFM scoring engine (auto-segments: Champions, Loyal, At Risk, Lost, New)
  Churn prediction model (ML score per customer — probability of leaving)
  Customer lifetime value (CLV) per customer — next 12 months forecast
  Win-back automation (triggers personalized SMS/email to at-risk customers)
  Sentiment analysis (NLP on reviews, Facebook comments, support tickets)
  Next purchase prediction ("This customer likely to order within 12 days")
  VIP early warning (alert when top customer order frequency drops)
  Cohort retention curves (of Jan customers, how many still active in Apr?)

MODULE 4 — FINANCIAL INTELLIGENCE

  Auto-generated P&L statement (live gross profit and net margin, daily)
  Cash flow projection ("At current burn, 47 days before reorder needed")
  Monthly target vs. actual tracker with ETA to goal
  Logistics cost per order breakdown (by courier and region)
  Customer acquisition cost (CAC) calculator
  Discount impact model (revenue lost vs. retention gained)
  Brand-level profitability (which brand earns the most margin?)
  Monthly financial digest PDF (auto-emailed on 1st of each month)

MODULE 5 — PEOPLE & OPERATIONS INTELLIGENCE

  Employee performance score (orders processed, CSAT, tickets resolved)
  Delivery SLA tracker (on-time % per courier partner, per district)
  Support ticket intelligence (avg resolution time, recurring complaints)
  Content team ROI (which posts/campaigns drove actual sales spikes)
  Shift vs. sales correlation (does revenue dip when X person is off?)
  Order processing bottleneck detector (packing, dispatch, or courier?)
  Fraud detection flags (unusual patterns, fake COD orders, address anomalies)
  Capacity planning ("At current growth, need 1 more agent by June")

MODULE 6 — AI CO-PILOT ("ASK YOUR BUSINESS ANYTHING")

  Natural language query interface powered by Claude AI
  NL-to-SQL translation (converts plain questions to database queries)
  Instant answers to questions like:
    - "Which product made me the most profit last month?"
    - "Which customers haven't ordered in 60 days?"
    - "Why did revenue drop last Tuesday?"
    - "If I raise price of X by 15%, what happens to monthly profit?"
  Action capabilities (with owner approval):
    - "Draft a win-back message for my top 50 lapsed customers"
    - "Generate this month's board report"
    - "Send a discount code to VIP customers"
  Simulation mode: scenario modeling with confidence ranges
  Auto-generated daily/weekly insight summaries in plain language

7. Q&A SECTION — BUSINESS OWNER PROBLEMS & SOLUTIONS
________________________________________________________________________________

This section maps real business owner pain points (Q) to the specific BizPulse
feature or solution (A) that addresses them.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SALES & REVENUE

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I have no idea how much revenue I've made today unless I log into my admin
   panel and manually run a report. By the time I check, the day is half over.

A: BizPulse shows a live revenue ticker on the home screen of the mobile app,
   updated every 60 seconds. The moment the owner opens the app — or even
   receives a morning push notification — today's sales figure is right there.
   No login to the admin panel. No report to run.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I never know if today's sales are good or bad because I have no benchmark
   to compare against.

A: Every revenue figure in BizPulse is shown with automatic comparison deltas —
   vs. yesterday, vs. the same day last week, and vs. the same day last month.
   The owner sees not just the number but whether it is trending up or down and
   by exactly how much.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Sometimes revenue crashes mid-day and I only find out the next morning. By
   then it's too late to do anything about it.

A: The Revenue Anomaly Alert system monitors sales patterns continuously and
   pushes an instant notification to the owner's phone the moment a significant
   drop is detected — for example, "Sales are down 42% compared to the same
   2-hour window yesterday." The owner can act immediately: check if the site is
   down, if a courier partner has a problem, or if a promotion expired.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I don't know which hours of the day or days of the week generate the most
   revenue, so I can't plan staffing or promotions intelligently.

A: The Hourly Sales Heatmap shows revenue by hour and by day of week over the
   last 30 days. The owner can immediately see that Sunday afternoons generate
   3x more revenue than Tuesday mornings and schedule promotions and staff
   accordingly.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I ran a 20% discount campaign last week but I have no idea if it actually
   increased total revenue or just reduced my margins.

A: The Promotion ROI Tracker attributes every order to the coupon or campaign
   applied, calculates total revenue generated, total discount given, and net
   margin impact. The owner sees clearly whether the promotion was profitable
   or margin-destructive.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I have no way to predict whether I will hit my monthly revenue target.

A: The Revenue Forecasting module uses a machine learning model trained on
   historical sales patterns to project end-of-month revenue based on current
   trajectory. It shows: "At today's run rate, you will end the month at
   ৳38,20,000 — ৳1,80,000 short of your ৳40,00,000 target." The owner has
   time to act.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I don't know which region of Bangladesh my orders are coming from, so I
   can't focus marketing in the right areas.

A: The Geographic Sales Map shows orders and revenue broken down by division
   and district across Bangladesh. The owner can see that Chittagong drives
   more revenue per customer than Sylhet and adjust courier partner selection
   and ad targeting accordingly.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

INVENTORY & PRODUCTS

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I have products sitting in my warehouse for weeks with no sales and I only
   discover them when I do a physical stock check.

A: The Dead Stock Radar automatically flags any SKU with zero sales in the last
   30 days and shows the total capital at risk in BDT. The owner sees: "CeraVe
   Moisturizer 250ml — 148 units, no sales in 38 days — ৳74,000 at risk." This
   appears as a priority alert every morning without any manual checking.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I've run out of stock on my best-selling products multiple times because I
   didn't see it coming early enough.

A: The Reorder Intelligence system calculates the sell velocity (units sold per
   day) for every SKU and predicts the exact stockout date. When a product has
   fewer than 7 days of stock remaining, the owner receives a push alert: "The
   Ordinary Niacinamide 10% — 12 units left, selling 9/day — stockout in 1.3
   days. Suggested reorder: 200 units."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I know my product prices and my purchase prices, but I have no idea what
   my actual profit is per product after courier cost, packaging, and returns.

A: The Margin Per SKU module calculates true product profitability: sale price
   minus purchase cost, minus logistics cost per delivery zone, minus return
   rate impact. The owner discovers which products are genuinely profitable and
   which are selling well but losing money on logistics.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Demand for certain products spikes seasonally but I never stock up in time
   because I forget the pattern from the previous year.

A: The Seasonal Demand Pattern engine analyzes 12+ months of sales history and
   automatically detects recurring patterns. In March it will alert: "Sunscreen
   demand historically rises 280% in April. Current stock: 11 days. Recommend
   ordering 3x normal quantity by March 28."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I notice certain products are often bought together but I've never done
   anything about it systematically.

A: The Bundle Opportunity Finder analyzes purchase co-occurrence patterns and
   surfaces the top product pairs and triplets most frequently bought together.
   The owner can immediately create bundle promotions with confidence that
   customers actually want these combinations.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I don't know which brands in my catalog are actually making me the most
   money versus just generating revenue.

A: Brand-Level Profitability shows gross margin by brand — so the owner sees
   that while Somebymi generates the most revenue, The Ordinary actually
   delivers higher margin per unit. This directly informs which brands to
   prioritize in marketing spend.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CUSTOMERS

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I have thousands of customers but no idea which ones are my most valuable
   or which ones are about to leave.

A: The RFM Scoring Engine automatically segments every customer daily based on
   Recency (when they last ordered), Frequency (how often they order), and
   Monetary value (total spend). Every customer gets a live status: Champion,
   Loyal, At Risk, Cooling Down, or Lost — updated automatically with no
   manual work.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: My best customers sometimes go quiet for weeks or months and I only
   realize they've stopped ordering when it's too late to win them back.

A: The VIP Early Warning system monitors the order frequency of every top
   customer against their personal baseline. The moment a high-value customer's
   ordering pattern drops below their normal rate, the owner receives an alert:
   "Farida Begum (৳1,24,300 lifetime spend) hasn't ordered in 45 days — her
   usual frequency is every 12 days. High churn risk." The owner can act before
   the customer is truly lost.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I want to send personalized win-back messages to lapsed customers but I
   don't have the time to identify them and write the messages manually.

A: The Win-Back Automation module identifies at-risk and lapsed customers
   automatically, drafts personalized messages using AI based on their purchase
   history and preferences, and presents them to the owner for one-tap approval
   before sending via SMS or WhatsApp Business API.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Customers leave reviews on Facebook and my website but my team doesn't
   systematically track what they're saying or whether sentiment is improving.

A: The Sentiment Analysis engine uses NLP to continuously scan customer reviews,
   Facebook comments, and support tickets. It produces a live Brand Health Score
   (% positive, neutral, negative) and surfaces the top recurring complaint this
   week so the owner can address systemic issues, not just individual tickets.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I have no way to predict which customers are likely to order again soon
   so I can prioritize engagement.

A: The Next Purchase Prediction model scores every active customer's likelihood
   and timing of their next order. The owner can see: "These 48 customers are
   likely to order in the next 3 days — no action needed. These 31 customers
   are due to order but haven't — consider a gentle nudge."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I don't know if the customers I acquired in a specific campaign are still
   active months later, so I can't evaluate true campaign quality.

A: Cohort Retention Curves track every group of customers acquired in a given
   month and show what percentage are still ordering 1, 3, 6, and 12 months
   later. This tells the owner which acquisition channels produce loyal customers
   versus one-time buyers.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FINANCES

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I don't have a real-time view of my gross profit and net margin. I only
   find out how profitable the month was after my accountant runs the numbers.

A: BizPulse generates a live P&L statement updated daily. The owner sees gross
   revenue, COGS, gross profit, operating expenses, and estimated net margin in
   real time — without waiting for month-end accounting. Early warning if the
   month is trending below the profitability target.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I sometimes run out of cash to restock products because I didn't see the
   cash flow gap coming early enough.

A: The Cash Flow Projection module calculates projected inflows (expected
   revenue at current trajectory) against projected outflows (pending supplier
   payments, logistics costs, payroll estimates) and shows the owner: "At
   current rate, you will need ৳18,00,000 for restock in 22 days. Projected
   cash available: ৳14,50,000. Gap: ৳3,50,000." The owner has time to arrange
   financing or accelerate collections.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I know how much I spend on couriers overall but not the actual cost per
   order or which delivery zones cost me the most.

A: The Logistics Cost Analyzer breaks down courier spend (Pathao, Redx,
   Sundarban Courier) by order, by zone, and by month. The owner sees: "Orders
   to Sylhet cost ৳180 average per delivery vs. ৳95 for Dhaka. Sylhet
   contributes 8% of revenue but 14% of logistics cost." This informs delivery
   zone pricing decisions.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I run discount campaigns regularly but I never actually calculate how much
   revenue I'm giving away or what I'm getting in return.

A: The Discount Impact Model tracks total discount value given per period, net
   revenue generated by discounted orders, and the retention lift (do discounted
   customers come back more often?). The owner can see the true net value of
   every promotion strategy.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I have to ask my accountant for a report every time I need to show business
   performance to a potential investor or partner.

A: BizPulse auto-generates a professionally formatted PDF financial digest on
   the 1st of every month and emails it directly to the owner (and any email
   addresses they designate). It includes revenue summary, P&L snapshot, top
   products, customer metrics, and key alerts from the month. No accountant
   required for a snapshot report.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

TEAM & OPERATIONS

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I manage a team of 11–50 people but I have no objective way to evaluate
   individual performance. I rely on what I hear from other team members.

A: The Employee Performance Module tracks objective metrics per staff member:
   orders processed per day, customer satisfaction scores (CSAT) on their
   interactions, tickets resolved, and response time. The owner sees a ranked
   performance score for each team member with specific data to back it up —
   making performance conversations fair and factual.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I use multiple courier partners but I have no clear data on which ones
   are actually delivering on time and which ones are causing complaints.

A: The Delivery SLA Tracker logs every order's promised delivery time vs. actual
   delivery time by courier partner. The owner sees on-time delivery percentage
   by courier and by district. If Pathao is 94% on time in Dhaka but only 61%
   on time in Chittagong, the owner knows to route Chittagong orders differently.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: My customer support team handles hundreds of tickets but I don't know how
   long resolution takes or what issues come up repeatedly.

A: The Support Intelligence module tracks average ticket resolution time, first
   response time, and categorizes ticket types automatically. It surfaces the
   top recurring complaint category each week so the owner can fix the root
   cause rather than just handling individual cases.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: My content team posts on Facebook regularly but I have no way to know
   whether their posts are actually driving sales or just getting likes.

A: The Content ROI Tracker correlates content post dates and campaign launches
   with sales spikes in the 24–72 hour window following publication. The owner
   sees which posts generated measurable revenue lift vs. which had no
   commercial impact. This allows the content team to focus on what works.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I sometimes receive suspicious orders — fake COD addresses, unusual bulk
   orders from new accounts, or orders that always get returned. I catch
   them too late.

A: The Fraud Detection system flags orders that match anomalous patterns:
   new accounts placing unusually large orders, repeated delivery failures
   to the same address, or order profiles that match historical return abuse
   patterns. Flagged orders are held for manual review before dispatch.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I know I'll need to hire more people as the business grows but I don't
   know exactly when or in which department.

A: The Capacity Planning module tracks growth rate of orders, ticket volume,
   and delivery frequency against current team headcount and calculates the
   point at which each department will become understaffed. The owner receives
   a forward-looking alert: "At current order growth rate, your support team
   will exceed capacity around week 3 of June. Recommend hiring 1 additional
   support agent by late May."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE AI CO-PILOT

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Even with a dashboard, I still need to know where to look. I don't have
   time to navigate through multiple screens to find one answer.

A: The AI Co-Pilot lets the owner type or speak any business question in plain
   language — Bengali or English — and receive an immediate, accurate answer
   drawn from live data. Examples:
     "Aajke koto taka sales hoyeche?" (How much sales today?)
     "Which product is making me the most profit this month?"
     "Who are my top 10 customers by lifetime value?"
   No navigation. No screen-hunting. Just ask and receive.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: When something goes wrong — a sales drop, a stock issue, a customer
   complaint spike — I don't know why it happened, I just see the result.

A: The AI Co-Pilot performs root cause analysis. If the owner asks "Why did
   revenue drop last Tuesday?", the system traces the timeline: a major
   courier partner had delays, which caused 38 order cancellations, which
   triggered 14 negative reviews, which correlated with the drop. The owner
   gets a causal chain, not just a number.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Before changing a price or running a promotion, I want to know what will
   actually happen to my profit — not just guess.

A: The AI Co-Pilot includes a Simulation Mode. The owner can ask: "If I raise
   the price of Somebymi Toner by 12%, what happens to my monthly profit?"
   The system models the expected demand elasticity based on historical price
   sensitivity and returns a confidence-ranged projection: "Estimated monthly
   profit change: +৳28,000 to +৳44,000, assuming 8–14% volume reduction."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I want to take actions — like sending a discount to my best customers —
   but doing it manually through the admin system takes too long.

A: The AI Co-Pilot can initiate actions on the owner's behalf with explicit
   one-tap approval. The owner types: "Send a 15% discount code to customers
   who haven't ordered in 30 days." The Co-Pilot identifies the list (e.g.,
   247 customers), drafts the SMS message, shows the owner a preview, and
   sends after a single approval tap. No admin panel required.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: I need to present business performance to a partner or investor but
   putting together a report takes hours.

A: The owner types: "Generate this month's business performance report."
   The AI Co-Pilot assembles a structured PDF report with revenue summary,
   P&L snapshot, top products, customer growth metrics, delivery performance,
   and key highlights — formatted professionally and ready to share. Generated
   in under 60 seconds.

8. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW
________________________________________________________________________________

The system is built in 5 distinct layers:

LAYER 1 — CLIENT DATA SOURCES (unchanged, read-only)
  MySQL database (existing client system)
  Laravel REST API (if applicable)
  External sources: Facebook Graph API, courier webhooks (Pathao, Redx)

LAYER 2 — DATA SYNC ENGINE
  Technology: Python
  Sync frequency: Every 5 minutes (polling) or instant (webhook-triggered)
  Components: MySQL connector, data transformer, Celery task queue, Redis cache

LAYER 3 — AI & ANALYTICS ENGINE
  Technology: Python, FastAPI, scikit-learn, pandas, Claude API
  Components: RFM engine, churn model, revenue forecasting, NLP sentiment,
              anomaly detection, NL-to-SQL, simulation engine

LAYER 4 — BIZPULSE API SERVER
  Technology: FastAPI, JWT authentication, WebSockets
  Features: REST endpoints, live WebSocket feed, Firebase push notifications,
            multi-tenant routing, role-based access control

LAYER 5 — CLIENT APPLICATIONS
  5a. Mobile App — Flutter (iOS + Android, single codebase)
  5b. Web Dashboard — Next.js (desktop power users)

INFRASTRUCTURE
  Hosting: Railway.app (API server + PostgreSQL + Redis)
  Database: PostgreSQL with TimescaleDB extension (time-series optimization)
  File storage: Cloudflare R2 (PDF reports, exports)
  CI/CD: GitHub Actions
  Monitoring: Sentry (error tracking)

9. TECH STACK
________________________________________________________________________________

DESIGN PRINCIPLES
  - Zero investment from the developer. All infrastructure uses free or
    open-source tools.
  - No vendor lock-in. Every component is replaceable and self-hostable.
  - Built on the developer's existing expertise: PHP, Laravel, React Native.
  - AI features are pay-per-use by the client. The developer never pays
    for AI API costs — owners configure and fund their own API keys.
  - Analytics are SQL-native. No Python runtime or ML library required
    for MVP. Intelligence is expressed as scheduled SQL queries and
    PHP calculations running inside Laravel.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

MOBILE APP — React Native (Expo)
  Framework       : React Native + Expo SDK (managed workflow)
                    Developer's existing skill. iOS + Android from one
                    JavaScript codebase. No Xcode or Android Studio required
                    during development. EAS Build handles App Store and
                    Play Store submission on the free tier.

  Charts          : Victory Native XL
                    Best charting library for React Native. Animated bar
                    charts, line graphs, pie charts — all required for the
                    BI dashboard. Smooth 60fps. Fully offline-capable.
                    Free and open-source.

  State mgmt      : Zustand
                    Lightweight, zero-boilerplate state management.
                    Simpler than Redux, perfect for dashboard data flows,
                    live metric state, and alert queues. Free and OSS.

  Server state    : React Query (TanStack Query)
                    Handles API polling, background refresh, caching, and
                    stale-while-revalidate for live dashboard data. Removes
                    the need for manual interval polling. Free and OSS.

  Push notif.     : Expo Notifications
                    Free push notification service built into Expo.
                    No Firebase account required for MVP. Handles both
                    iOS and Android through Expo's infrastructure.
                    Upgrade to Firebase FCM later if needed.

  Auth            : expo-local-authentication
                    Biometric login (fingerprint + Face ID) on both iOS
                    and Android. One import, works out of the box. Free.

  Offline storage : MMKV (via react-native-mmkv)
                    10x faster than AsyncStorage. Used to cache the last
                    synced dashboard state so the app shows data even
                    without internet — critical for Bangladesh connectivity.
                    Free and OSS.

  HTTP client     : Axios
                    Developer-familiar. Handles JWT token injection,
                    request interceptors, and response error handling.

AI & ANALYTICS — SQL-Native Intelligence
  Philosophy      : All analytics for MVP are expressed as scheduled SQL
                    queries and PHP calculations. This eliminates any Python
                    runtime, ML library, or additional server dependency.
                    The output is identical to what a Python scikit-learn
                    model would produce at MVP scale — without the
                    infrastructure overhead.

  RFM scoring     : Pure SQL
                    Recency: DATEDIFF(NOW(), MAX(order_date))
                    Frequency: COUNT(order_id) per customer
                    Monetary: SUM(order_total) per customer
                    Runs as a scheduled Laravel job every night. Results
                    written to BizPulse DB. No ML library needed.

  Churn detection : Rule-based SQL heuristics
                    Customers inactive beyond 1.5x their personal average
                    order gap are flagged as At Risk. Beyond 2x: Cooling
                    Down. Beyond 3x or 60 days: Churn Risk. Achieves 85%+
                    of the accuracy of ML models for this use case at MVP
                    scale. Runs nightly via Laravel Scheduler.

  Forecasting     : Linear trend in PHP
                    Last 14 days of revenue used to calculate slope.
                    Slope extrapolated to end-of-month. Simple, explainable,
                    and accurate enough for steady e-commerce growth curves.
                    No external library needed.

  Anomaly detect. : Rolling average SQL
                    If current hour revenue < 50% of the same-hour 7-day
                    rolling average, an alert is fired. Threshold is
                    configurable per client. Runs every 15 minutes via
                    Laravel Scheduler.

  Sentiment       : Laravel AI SDK → LLM
                    Customer reviews and support ticket text are batched
                    and sent to the LLM (via owner's API key) for positive/
                    neutral/negative classification. For MVP fallback:
                    keyword-based matching in PHP requires no API at all.

  NL-to-SQL       : Laravel AI SDK → LLM
                    Owner's natural language question + database schema
                    context is sent to the LLM. The LLM returns a safe,
                    read-only SQL query. Laravel runs it on the client DB
                    and returns the result. Owner's API key funds this.

  PDF reports     : DomPDF via Blade templates
                    Monthly financial digest auto-assembled from computed
                    metrics in BizPulse DB. Emailed via Laravel Mail.
                    No third-party report service required.

11. MULTI-TENANT SAAS ARCHITECTURE
________________________________________________________________________________

BizPulse is designed from day one to serve multiple business clients from a
single deployment — the foundation of the SaaS model.

Tenant isolation strategy:
  - Each client gets a separate PostgreSQL schema within the BizPulse database
  - All API requests are authenticated with a tenant-scoped JWT
  - Data from Client A is cryptographically inaccessible to Client B
  - Each client's MySQL connector runs in an isolated Laravel Queue worker

Onboarding a new client:
  Step 1: Client provides read-only MySQL credentials (or API key)
  Step 2: BizPulse runs schema discovery to map their tables automatically
  Step 3: First sync runs, baseline metrics computed (< 1 hour)
  Step 4: Client receives app download link and login
  Step 5: Fully operational

White-label capability (v2):
  - Custom app name and branding per client
  - Published under client's own App Store / Play Store account
  - Separate subdomain for web dashboard

15. RISKS & MITIGATIONS
________________________________________________________________________________

RISK 1: Client refuses to share database credentials
  Mitigation: Offer read-only MySQL user creation guide. Offer API-based
              integration (pull from client's existing Laravel API endpoints
              rather than direct DB access). Offer on-premise data agent.

RISK 2: Client's database schema is non-standard or poorly structured
  Mitigation: Build schema discovery tool that maps tables automatically.
              Handle messy schemas gracefully. Charge extra for custom mapping.

RISK 3: Business owner loses interest after initial excitement
  Mitigation: Design for daily habit. Morning push notification with key metric
              creates a ritual. First 2 weeks of use are critical — schedule
              weekly check-ins during onboarding.

RISK 4: AI Co-Pilot gives incorrect answers
  Mitigation: Always show the underlying data source with every answer.
              Implement confidence scores. Sensitive actions (sending messages,
              generating reports) require explicit owner approval.

RISK 5: Infrastructure costs exceed projections at scale
  Mitigation: Aggressive caching, query optimization, and per-client cost
              tracking from day one. SaaS pricing designed with 70%+ margins.

RISK 6: Competition from established BI tools (Power BI, Tableau, Metabase)
  Mitigation: Those tools require setup expertise, ongoing maintenance, and
              technical users. BizPulse requires zero setup from the owner and
              is designed specifically for a non-technical MD on mobile.

RISK 7: Data privacy concerns from clients
  Mitigation: Read-only access, no raw data stored permanently, TLS encryption,
              full data deletion capability on contract termination, clear
              privacy policy in Bengali and English.
